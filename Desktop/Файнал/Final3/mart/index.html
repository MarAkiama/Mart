About us

Mart is a Qazaq multinational clothing-retail company, known for its fast-fashion clothing for men, women, teenagers and children. Mart and its associated brands operate in 62 countries with over 4,500 stores and, as of 2015, employ around 132,000 people. It is the second largest global clothing retailer, just behind Spain-based Inditex (parent company of Zara). The company has a significant online presence, with online shopping available in 33 countries, COS in 19 countries, Monki and Weekday in 18 countries each, Other Stories in 13 countries, and Cheap Monday in 5 countries.
